书画家介绍 - Artist Introduction

个人简历 - Curriculum Vitae

姓名：李军 - Li Jun
性别：男 - Male
出生年月：1974年 - 1974

作为柳州美术家协会会员，我投身国画艺术领域多年，在创作与教学方面均取得优异成果，对国画艺术满怀热忱与执着，不断追求艺术造诣的提升。

As a member of the Liuzhou Artists Association, I have been involved in the field of Chinese painting art for many years. I have achieved outstanding results in both creation and teaching. I am full of enthusiasm and perseverance in Chinese painting art, and constantly pursue the improvement of my artistic accomplishment.

教育背景 - Education

先后跟随刘文威老师和欧顺红老师学习国画，在名师的悉心指导下，深入研习国画的笔墨技法、构图理论和艺术内涵，为扎实的国画功底奠定了基础。

After that, he followed Liu Wenwei and Ou Shunhong to study Chinese painting. Under the careful guidance of famous teachers, he deeply studied the brush and ink techniques, composition theory and artistic connotation of Chinese painting, which laid the foundation for a solid foundation of Chinese painting.

工作经历 - Work Experience

多年来专注于国画教学工作，凭借丰富的教学经验与独特的教学方法，激发学生对国画的兴趣，培养他们的艺术素养和创作能力。

Focusing on the teaching of Chinese painting for many years, with rich teaching experience and unique teaching methods, it stimulates students' interest in Chinese painting and cultivates their artistic literacy and creative ability.

获奖荣誉 - Award-winning Accolades

荣获2023、2024届广西区优秀国画指导教师奖，该荣誉不仅是对我教学工作的高度认可，也激励我在国画教育领域不断探索创新，为培养更多优秀的国画人才贡献力量。

Won the 2023 and 2024 Guangxi District Excellent Chinese Painting Teacher Awards, this honor is not only a high recognition of my teaching work, but also inspires me to continue to explore and innovate in the field of Chinese painting education, and contribute to the cultivation of more outstanding Chinese painting talents.

个人技能 - Personal Skills

熟练掌握小，大写意等多种国画技法，擅长山水、花鸟等题材的创作，作品多次在地方展览中展出并获得好评。能够将传统国画技艺与现代审美相结合，创作出富有时代气息的作品。具备出色的教学能力，善于因材施教，引导学生挖掘自身艺术潜力。

Proficient in small and large Chinese painting techniques, good at creating landscapes, flowers and birds and other subjects. His works have been exhibited in local exhibitions many times and have won praise.It can combine traditional Chinese painting techniques with modern aesthetics to create works with a rich sense of the times.Has excellent teaching ability, good at teaching, guide students to explore their artistic potential.
